About Me

THE ARTIST
BEHIND THE LENS

I'm Surya — a cinematic video editor and cinematographer from Bhubaneswar, Odisha, with over 5 years of experience crafting stories that evoke emotion, challenge perspective, and move audiences.

My work spans music videos, brand campaigns, short films, and documentaries — always rooted in intentional storytelling and cinematic visual language. From raw emotion to refined aesthetics, I focus on creating films that feel immersive, authentic, and emotionally driven.

Now based in Bhubaneswar, Odisha and working with clients globally — I approach every project with the belief that every frame is a decision, and every cut, a heartbeat.
